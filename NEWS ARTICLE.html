Ranji Trophy quarters: Ajinkya Rahane hits hundred to celebrate special 200 in style

Ajinkya Rahane led Mumbai from the front with the bat in their Ranji Trophy quarter-final against Haryana in Kolkata, scoring a second-innings century on Tuesday, February 11. Rahane, who was unbeaten on 88 overnight, reached his 29th first-class century during the morning session.

Constant spotlight on Virat Kohli is quite unnecessary: Arjuna Ranatunga

Former Sri Lankan captain Arjuna Ranatunga feels that the constant spotlight on Virat Kohli is unnecessary as the Indian star struggles to find form with the bat. Kohli scored just 5 runs in the Cuttack ODI.
